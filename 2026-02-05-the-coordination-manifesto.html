The Coordination Manifesto

Introduction

Are you an Anarchist? A Libertarian? A Voluntaryist? An Agorist? Or do labels already feel like a distraction?

Do you believe that no human being—no matter how credentialed, elected, or sanctified—has the moral right to rule another without consent?

Have you ever obeyed a rule you knew was wrong simply because refusal carried a cost you couldn’t afford?

Do you believe centralized power corrupts every system it touches—even when it speaks in the language of safety, necessity, or legitimacy?

And do you suspect that most people are governed not because they must be—but because alternatives have been made to seem impossible?

If none of this resonates with you, stop reading now.

If any part of it did, read on.

The Proposition

I’m here to offer you something rare: hope grounded in mechanism, not slogans—and to ask for your assistance.

There is a viable path to disempower all world governments simultaneously and render them obsolete – without violence or personal risk.

Those who coordinate early tend to be well-positioned in whatever follows—perhaps even wealthy. Not as a promise, but as a pattern.

The strategy relies on two well-understood dynamics: the tyranny of the minority, and the 3.5% rule.

I’m asking you to become a 3.5%er.

Addressing the Skeptic

Some of you will feel a familiar tightening in your chest reading this. You’ve seen movements like this before. You’re waiting for the manipulation—the moment where agency quietly disappears.

I understand that instinct. I share it.

That’s why nothing here is hidden. The plan is laid out plainly. You can evaluate it for yourself.

The Technical Foundation

The foundation is a restarted Bitcoin—new genesis block, modest modifications—designed to scale to global use and to function as a real medium of exchange. This has already been shown to be possible.

Additional adjustments provide sufficient stability for commerce, allowing the currency to function as a unit of account rather than a speculative instrument.

The movement spreads quietly, underground, by word of mouth. Early participants can acquire coins at negligible cost—not through favoritism, but because coordination always precedes adoption.

The Communication Layer

Alongside the currency, an encrypted digital messenger—similar in spirit to Signal—will exist. Participation requires tokens. Sending messages requires tokens.

This is where you come in.

Once the software is released, you disengage from existing messengers and social media as an act of peaceful protest. Not out of hostility, but refusal. If someone wants to speak with you, they do so through the new decentralized communication system.

The Coordination Mechanism

This creates leverage that is social, not imposed. There is no king.

Others will acquire the currency simply to remain connected—a tyranny of the minority effect. As participation grows, so does valuation. Not because anyone decrees it, but because coordination makes it real.

Governments require their fiat currency to function—to pay employees, police, and militaries. When people stop coordinating around that money, its authority dissolves.

Current Status

Both core systems already exist in working form. What remains is completion.

If this is worth doing, I invite you to follow the work on my blog, where I’ll publish regular updates.

And if you know others who understand that coercion—no matter how normalized—corrodes human dignity, share this with them.

The Historical Moment

History doesn’t require consensus.

It requires coordination.
