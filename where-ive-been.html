Where I’ve Been

Shammah Chancellor — February 2026

If you subscribed to this Patreon and then quietly unsubscribed while I went silent, I understand. I want to explain what happened.

In late 2022, my house flooded during a major storm. The damage was significant and the recovery took most of the following year. Around the same time, a long-term relationship I had been in ended. Shortly after that, I lost my job. These things did not happen one at a time — they compressed into a period of about eighteen months that tested everything I had.

Lotus did not get finished. That is the honest answer to where the project went. I had it 90% complete, and then my life required my full attention just to stay intact.

What I want you to know is that I kept working when I could. The theoretical foundation is solid. Three research papers are published and freely available at givelotus.github.io, translated into five languages. The protocol works. The math holds. That part never stopped being true.

I am still here. I am getting back on my feet and I intend to finish what I started. I am not asking you to take that on faith indefinitely — I am asking for the chance to show you over the coming months that this project is real and that I am still the person who can see it through.

If you want to support that, the Patreon is still open. If you want to follow along without committing, the research is public and always will be. And if you walked away and don’t come back, I understand that too.

Thank you for having believed in this at all.

— Shammah
